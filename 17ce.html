17th Century

The 17th century was dominated by scientific breakthroughs, global trade expansion, and devastating wars in Europe.

  • Major Events
    1. Scientific Revolution
    2. Establishment of global trading companies (VOC, EIC)
    3. Colonization of North America
  • Major Empires
    1. Ottoman Empire
    2. Mughal Empire
    3. Spanish Empire
    4. Dutch and English colonial empires
  • Major Conflicts
    1. Thirty Years’ War (1618–1648)
    2. English Civil War
    3. Ottoman–Safavid conflicts
  • Population Trends
    1. Slow recovery from plague outbreaks
    2. Growth of Atlantic colonies
  • Key Leaders
    1. Louis XIV of France
    2. Shah Jahan
    3. Tokugawa Ieyasu
  • Key Intellectuals
    1. Isaac Newton
    2. René Descartes
    3. Galileo Galilei
  • Major Religions
    1. Christianity (Catholic & Protestant)
    2. Islam
    3. Buddhism in East Asia
  • Currency
    1. Dutch Guilder
    2. English Pound Sterling
