1st Century BCE Overview

1st Century BCE

The 1st century BCE saw the rise of the Roman Empire and major political upheavals across the Mediterranean.

  • Major Events
    1. Julius Caesar's rise and assassination (44 BCE)
    2. End of Roman Republic, start of Roman Empire
  • Major Empires
    1. Roman Republic → Roman Empire
    2. Parthian Empire
  • Major Conflicts
    1. Gallic Wars (58–50 BCE)
    2. Roman Civil Wars
  • Population Trends
    1. Urbanization in Rome, Alexandria, Antioch
  • Key Leaders
    1. Julius Caesar
    2. Pompey
    3. Mark Antony
  • Key Intellectuals
    1. Cicero (Philosophy/Politics)
  • Major Religions
    1. Roman Religion, Hellenistic Polytheism, Early Judaism
  • Currency
    1. Roman Denarius
