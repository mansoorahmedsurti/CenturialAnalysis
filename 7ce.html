7th Century Overview

7th Century

The 7th century witnessed the rise of Islam, major imperial transitions, and the reshaping of Eurasian power structures.

  • Major Events
    1. Rise of Islam (610 CE)
    2. Hijra to Medina (622 CE)
    3. Fall of Sassanian Empire
  • Major Empires
    1. Byzantine Empire
    2. Rashidun → Umayyad Caliphate
    3. Tang Dynasty
  • Major Conflicts
    1. Byzantine–Sassanian Wars
    2. Early Islamic conquests
  • Population Trends
    1. Urban expansion in Arabia and Persia
    2. Prosperity in Tang China
  • Key Leaders
    1. Prophet Muhammad
    2. Emperor Taizong
    3. Caliphs Abu Bakr, Umar, Uthman, Ali
  • Key Intellectuals
    1. Xuanzang
    2. Hasan al-Basri
  • Major Religions
    1. Islam
    2. Christianity
    3. Buddhism (East Asia)
  • Currency
    1. Byzantine Solidus
    2. Early Islamic Dinar
