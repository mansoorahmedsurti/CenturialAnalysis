2nd Century CE Overview

2nd Century CE

The 2nd century CE is known for the Roman Empire's territorial peak and cultural prosperity.

  • Major Events
    1. Pax Romana continues under the "Five Good Emperors"
    2. Expansion into Dacia and Arabia Petraea
  • Major Empires
    1. Roman Empire
    2. Parthian Empire
  • Major Conflicts
    1. Roman–Parthian wars
    2. Jewish–Roman wars continue
  • Population Trends
    1. Urban and trade growth across the Mediterranean
  • Key Leaders
    1. Trajan
    2. Hadrian
  • Key Intellectuals
    1. Galen (Medicine/Philosophy)
  • Major Religions
    1. Roman Religion, Early Christianity
  • Currency
    1. Roman Denarius, Aureus
