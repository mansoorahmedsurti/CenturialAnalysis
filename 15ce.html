15th Century

The 15th century marked the transition into the early modern world, driven by exploration, printing, and rising empires.

  • Major Events
    1. Fall of Constantinople (1453 CE)
    2. Gutenberg's printing press
    3. Age of Exploration begins
  • Major Empires
    1. Ottoman Empire
    2. Ming Dynasty
    3. Spanish Empire
    4. Portuguese Empire
  • Major Conflicts
    1. Ottoman expansion
    2. End of Hundred Years’ War (1453)
    3. Wars of the Roses
  • Population Trends
    1. Recovery from the Black Death
    2. Urban growth in Renaissance Italy
  • Key Leaders
    1. Mehmed II
    2. Ferdinand and Isabella
    3. Henry VII
  • Key Intellectuals
    1. Leonardo da Vinci
    2. Michelangelo
    3. Gutenberg
  • Major Religions
    1. Christianity
    2. Islam
  • Currency
    1. Venetian Ducat
    2. Ottoman Akçe
